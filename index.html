About me

I am a freelance photographer with over 3 years experience in the field. My passion has always been about capturing the magic moments that will make my clients smile every time they are looking at their photograph. Therefore, I love to look through the lens of my camera constantly seeking for the perfect shot.

Whether I am standing in front of a breathtaking scenery in Yosemite National Park or witness the radiant smiles of an entire family at the peak of happiness, everything is magical and priceless through my eyes.

During Lunar New Year of 2018, I had a chance to take pictures for a Vietnamese temple during their New Year festival. It was an opportunity for me to appreciate and maintain the cultural tradition of my country, Vietnam. I was in awe to see one of the most beautiful cherry blossom tree in the garden of Pho Tu temple in Hayward.

Immediately, I took many photos of the flowers and it turns out so perfect. The cherry flowers blossom during spring bringing a new sense of tranquility for everyone. Please take a look at it in my Portfolio page. If you like please leave me comment and feedback. Perhaps, your thoughts could help me improve my skills furthermore.
